Q1. Can I trust what you are publishing?
Yes. All guidance is evidence-based and reviewed by a Scientific Advisory Board. The charity is also overseen by a Board of Trustees to ensure compliance with regulatory standards.

Q2. What does the Active Pregnancy Foundation do?
The Active Pregnancy Foundation supports women to stay active during pregnancy and beyond by providing evidence-based guidance for women, fitness professionals, and healthcare professionals. The charity aims to challenge myths and promote informed, active lifestyles.
