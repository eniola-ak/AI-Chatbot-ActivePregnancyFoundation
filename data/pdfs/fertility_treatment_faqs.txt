Q1. Is it safe to be active during fertility treatment?
If you are already moderately active, you can usually continue during fertility treatment if you feel comfortable. Activity levels may need adjusting depending on how your body responds.

Q2. What does research say about being active during fertility treatment?
Research shows mixed findings. Moderate activity may have positive effects, while being inactive or extremely active may negatively impact outcomes. Many women already engage in moderate activity, which is generally encouraged.

Q3. Are there any risks associated with activity during fertility treatment?
Certain conditions may increase the risk of ovarian torsion, particularly during fertility treatment. During stimulation phases, heavy lifting and twisting movements are not recommended. Women are encouraged to make informed choices and consult healthcare professionals when unsure.
